Results

Reflex-o delivers an intuitive, playful experience that fits perfectly on the wrist. The combination of voice commands, real-time animations, and haptic feedback creates an engaging game loop.

Users praised the quick pick-up-and-play nature of the game and its satisfying tactile feedback system.

September 2025

Solution

Reflexo uses SwiftUI for fluid UI components, AVFoundation for crisp voice prompts, and WatchKit haptics for tactile feedback.

Voice Commands

Randomized Bop It, Twist It, and Shake It commands keep players on their toes.

Haptic Feedback

WatchKit haptics provide satisfying tactile responses for every action.

Real-time Animations

SwiftUI delivers smooth, responsive visual feedback during gameplay.

July 2025

Problem

Smartwatch games often struggle with limited screen real estate, unreliable audio integration, and lackluster feedback loops.

The challenge was to create a game that felt fun, quick to pick up, and perfectly sized for the wrist.

May 2025

Project Started

Reflexo is a fast-paced Apple Watch game designed to test user reflexes using voice commands, real-time animations, and haptic feedback.

Built entirely in SwiftUI and AVFoundation, it delivers an intuitive, playful experience that fits perfectly on the wrist.
