Results & Reception

Old York Times has been exceptionally well received from beta testers and early users. The site's professional design, which skillfully marries the vintage aesthetic with modern digital features, has resonated with users.

It has successfully engaged audiences with its dual approach to current news and fictional storytelling, leading to increased user interaction and enhanced brand presence.

August 2024

The Problem

Looking at current news and article sites, they seem to have become less expressive and creative for users, with some even adding unwanted complexity to their design.

The challenge was to craft a digital experience that captures the essence of old pulp fiction and 1930s news while meeting today's standards for usability.

June 2024

Project Started

The Old York Times website was built to evoke the timeless charm of pulp fiction and classic journalism, redefining news delivery for modern audiences.

Designed to engage users with both current news and fictional stories, blending a nostalgic aesthetic with modern interactive features.
