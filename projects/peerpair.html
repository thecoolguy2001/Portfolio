October 2023

The Solution

A mobile app that utilizes TikTok's API to gather user data and creates a matching algorithm to connect users with similar interests.

Content-Based Matching

Matches users based on the TikTok content they engage with, ensuring shared interests.

Fun Experience

Gamified interface makes discovering new connections enjoyable and engaging.

Privacy First

Users control what data is shared and can disconnect their TikTok at any time.

August 2023

The Problem

The challenge was to develop an app that could effectively match users based on their TikTok activity, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable experience.

Traditional matching apps rely on self-reported interests, which can be inaccurate. Content consumption patterns provide a more authentic view of someone's personality.

July 2023

Project Started

The development of the PeerPair app aimed to create a unique platform that matches users based on their TikTok activity and preferences.

The project focused on leveraging user-generated content to facilitate connections and provide a fun and engaging user experience.
