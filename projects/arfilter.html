June 2025

Problem

Most TikTok filters are either purely visual or static. Users engage with them briefly but rarely return. There's a missed opportunity to turn filters into repeatable micro games.

The challenge was creating AR experiences that users would want to play multiple times and share with friends.

June 2025

Project Started

The Amaze Games AR Filter TikTok series is a collection of interactive TikTok game filters built using Effect House.

These augmented reality (AR) games are designed to engage users with fast, reflex-based challenges that blend nostalgia, humor, and social competitiveness.
