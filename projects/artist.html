Results & Reception

The artist's portfolio website was delivered with strong visual impact and interactive elements that captivated visitors. The Three.js integrations created an immersive 3D experience that set the portfolio apart from traditional artist websites.

Through this project, I learned how to effectively translate a client's vision into a tangible online presence. The experience taught me the importance of close collaboration and iterative design.
