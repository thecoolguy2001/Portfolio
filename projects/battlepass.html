July 2025

Visual Design Direction

Battle Pass leans into game-boy / handheld nostalgia — pixel-art glyphs, stat bars, and chunky type. The visual language signals "game" the moment a Flipper boots into it.

Pixel-art avatar

Each player is represented by a small monochrome sprite. Stats determine subtle differences in posture so duels feel personalized.

Bars over numbers

Stats render as horizontal bars. Battles play out visually — bars drain in real time so the round feels like a fight, not a calculation.

Encounter notification

Detection of another Flipper plays a short buzzer sting and flashes the screen — the device demands attention without lifting the user out.

Verdict animation

Win, loss, draw each get a distinct screen sequence — half-second of theater that makes the result land.

July 2025

Solution

Battle Pass turns every Wi-Fi scan into a chance for a quick-flash Flipper duel. Each user sets a nickname and hidden stats (strength, speed, defense), which are broadcast via the Wi-Fi devboard.

Custom Profile

Set your nickname and allocate stats for strength, speed, and defense.

Auto-Detection

Automatically detects nearby Flipper devices running Battle Pass.

Quick Battles

Instant stats-based duels resolve automatically on encounter.

June 2025

Target Users

Battle Pass is built for the social side of the Flipper community — people who already attend meetups, conferences, and hardware events where Flippers are commonly carried.

CG

The Conference Goer

Brings their Flipper to DEFCON, hardware meetups, hackerspaces

Goals

  • Discover other Flipper users in the wild
  • Lightweight social signal at events
  • Souvenir of a real-world encounter

Pain Points

  • No native way to find other Flipper users nearby
  • Most peer apps require manual pairing
SN

The StreetPass Nostalgic

Misses Nintendo's StreetPass and wants that feeling back

Goals

  • Passive multiplayer that respects their time
  • Surprise rewards from walking around
  • A growing log of encounters to flip through

Pain Points

  • StreetPass is dead, nothing has replaced it
  • Modern multiplayer demands constant attention

June 2025

Problem

The Flipper Zero community thrives on interaction and creativity, but most peer-to-peer activity requires active input or hacking modules. There's no playful, automatic way for Flipper owners to recognize and battle each other in the wild.

The challenge was creating a social gaming experience using passive wireless scanning.

June 2025

Project Started

Battle Pass is a Flipper Zero app that turns passive encounters into live, stats-based battles, bringing a nostalgic, Nintendo-inspired twist to real-world interactions.

Designed for Wi-Fi devboard scanning, the app detects nearby Flipper devices running the same app and initiates automatic battles.
