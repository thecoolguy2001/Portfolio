Solution

Truth Detector uses a custom DetectorSession to record a stable baseline heart rate, then streams live HealthKit data during a questioning phase.

Baseline Capture

Records stable heart rate to establish a personal baseline for comparison.

Live Monitoring

Streams real-time HealthKit data during the questioning phase.

Result Mapping

Computes BPM changes and maps deltas to Truth, Uncertain, or Lie results.

July 2025

Problem

Users are intrigued by polygraphs but lack accessible, personal tools to explore biometric feedback.

The challenge was making biometric data feel emotional and entertaining rather than clinical.

May 2025

Project Started

Fibber is an Apple Watch app that simulates a lie-detector by leveraging heart-rate data to infer truthfulness.

Using HealthKit and SwiftUI, it guides users through a baseline capture, live monitoring during questioning, and then visualizes results.
