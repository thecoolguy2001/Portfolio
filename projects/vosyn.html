May 2024

Results & Reception

The redesign received highly positive feedback from both the team and company employees. The new design significantly improved the overall aesthetic and user experience.

The enhanced careers page drove a higher number of job applicants, and the improved call-to-action elements boosted user sign-ups. Overall, the project successfully elevated Vosyn's online presence.

April 2024

Design Iterations

The redesign focused on three key areas to maximize impact.

v3.0

Accessibility Enhancement

Optimized page layout, improved readability, and ensured compliance with accessibility standards.

v2.0

Careers Page Redesign

Added clear, compelling information about culture, open positions, and application process with eye-catching visuals.

v1.0

Call-to-Action Improvements

Streamlined content, made buttons more prominent, and strategically placed CTAs to maximize conversions.

02

03

04

Presented redesigned website to employees and gathered feedback to refine the design.

Modern Design

February 2024

The Problem

Vosyn's previous website lacked the modern design elements and user-friendly features necessary to attract and retain users.

Main issues included ineffective call-to-actions, outdated design aesthetics, and insufficient information on key pages like careers and accessibility.

February 2024

Project Started

The redesign of the Vosyn website aimed to elevate the tech startup's website with industry-leading design and enhanced user engagement.

The project focused on creating visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces, driving user sign-ups, and increasing interest in products and career opportunities.
