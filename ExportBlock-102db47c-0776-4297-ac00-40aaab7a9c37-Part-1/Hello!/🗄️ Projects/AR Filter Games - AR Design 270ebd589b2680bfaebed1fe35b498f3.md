# Summary

The Amaze Games AR Filter TikTok series is a collection of interactive TikTok game filters built using Effect House. These augmented reality (AR) games are designed to engage users with fast, reflex-based challenges that blends nostalgia, humor, and social competitiveness. This initiative is part of Amaze Games, a creative extension of the Amaze brand focused on delivering lightweight, viral-ready gaming experiences through social platforms.

I studied trending effects, user comments, and engagement metrics on TikTok game filters to determine what types of interaction drive the most traction.

# Major Design Iterations

### - Iteration 1.

Initial filters were too visual and lacked game logic. This version focused on aesthetics but failed to deliver lasting engagement.

### - Iteration 2.

I added game mechanics like timers, triggers, and scoring using Effect House scripting. Now users could win or lose, and content felt more interactive.

### - Iteration 3.

Introduced a consistent branded overlay, sound feedback, win/lose animations, and planned a content rollout strategy. Filters became shareable and replayable, and engagement noticeably increased.
