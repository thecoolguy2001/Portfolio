# Major Design Iterations

### - Iteration 1.

Amaze Web began as a direct response to early client needs brands and creators who needed polished, fast-turnaround websites without the high cost of long-term web retainers. I worked closely with these clients to understand their visual identity, brand goals, and pain points, building custom websites from scratch to match their vision.

### - Iteration 2.

After recognizing patterns in what most clients were asking for, I shifted toward building reusable, scalable solutions in the form of Framer and Shopify templates. These templates allowed me to offer faster, more affordable builds while still preserving the custom feel. Each theme was designed with creative flexibility and conversion in mind, reducing time-to-launch for both me and the client.

### - Iteration 3.

Currently, I‚Äôm expanding Amaze Web into service kits that go beyond design‚Äîintroducing AI-powered automation systems and fully designed email templates for platforms like Shopify Email. These offerings are built to give users powerful backend systems with the same plug-and-play simplicity as my themes, enabling creators and small brands to launch with both visual polish and operational efficiency.
